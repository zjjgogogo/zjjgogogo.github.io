Privacy Policy for DailySudoKu-MINI

Effective Date: March 2026
Developer: JamesJaw

Thank you for choosing DailySudoKu-MINI. We built this app to provide a peaceful, relaxing, and ad-free puzzle experience. We believe that true relaxation requires complete privacy. Therefore, our privacy policy is very simple: we do not collect your data.

1. Zero Data Collection

DailySudoKu-MINI does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics trackers, background tracking cookies, or crash reporting SDKs.
  • No Ads: There are no advertising networks integrated into this app.
  • 100% Local: All your gameplay progress, puzzle history, pencil notes, and app settings are stored strictly locally on your own device. We have absolutely no access to this data.

2. In-App Purchases (Tip Jar)

If you choose to support the development of this app through our "Tip Jar," please know that the transaction is processed securely and entirely by the Apple App Store.

  • We do not collect, process, or have access to your credit card information, billing address, or any personal financial details.
  • We only receive an anonymous confirmation receipt from Apple to unlock your supporter rewards (such as custom app icons).

3. Children's Privacy

Because our app operates entirely offline and collects absolutely zero personal data, it is completely safe for a general audience, including children under the age of 13.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we introduce new features in the future that require data collection (such as an online leaderboard), we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and request your explicit consent within the app. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
