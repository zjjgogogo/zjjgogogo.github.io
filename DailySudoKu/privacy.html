Privacy Policy for DailySudoKu-MINI

Effective Date: March 2026
Developer: JamesJaw

Thank you for choosing DailySudoKu-MINI. We built this app to provide a peaceful, relaxing, and ad-free puzzle experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

For our core, free gameplay experience, we do not collect any personal data.

  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics trackers, crash reporters, or advertising SDKs.
  • Local Storage: All your gameplay progress, pencil notes, and completed puzzles for the standard mode are stored locally on your device. We cannot see, read, or access this data.

2. Information We Collect (For Premium/Competitive Features)

If you choose to participate in our competitive mode ("Golden Nib Club" / Daily Leaderboard), we need to collect specific data to ensure the fairness of the global leaderboard:

  • Authentication Data: To prevent cheating and maintain your rank, we require you to sign in using third-party authentication services. We only receive a unique user identifier (User ID) and the display name you choose to share. We do not collect your raw email address unless you explicitly grant permission.
  • Gameplay Data: When you play a competitive daily puzzle, our servers record your start time, completion time, and puzzle seed to calculate your score for the leaderboard.

3. In-App Purchases and Financial Data

All in-app purchases and "Tip Jar" donations are securely processed by the Apple App Store.

  • We do not collect, process, or have access to your credit card information, billing address, or any other financial details.
  • We only receive an anonymous receipt token to verify your purchase and unlock the corresponding features or supporter rewards within the app.

4. How We Use Your Information

The minimal data we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the daily global leaderboard.
  • To verify premium subscriptions and prevent fraudulent score submissions.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Leaderboard data is tied to your User ID. If you wish to delete your account and remove your scores from our active leaderboards, you can request account deletion by contacting us directly.

6. Children's Privacy

DailySudoKu-MINI is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
