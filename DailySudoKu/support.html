Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Daily Puzzle work? ▼
Every day, a new puzzle is delivered to your app. The puzzle is the same for everyone around the world. Progress is saved locally on your device.
I bought a Tip/Coffee, how do I change my app icon? ▼
Thank you for your support! You can change your app icon and stamp color by going to the Settings menu (the gear icon) and tapping "Supporter Rewards".
How does the Golden Nib Club leaderboard work? ▼
To ensure fairness, the daily competitive puzzle uses server-side timing. Once you start, the timer cannot be paused. Make sure you have a stable internet connection when submitting your final score!
Can I restore my purchases if I change phones? ▼
Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Please note that local puzzle progress (for the free mode) is saved on your device and may not transfer automatically.
